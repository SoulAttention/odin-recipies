Tofu Recipe

Pan fried Tofu is delicous and it is good for you! It contains a lot of iron
and is made out of soy so it is also good for the planet.

Ingredients

  • Extra Firm Tofu
  • Green Onions
  • Oil
  • Honey
  • Spices

Directions

  1. Squeeze all water from tofu using a paper towl
  2. Heat up oil in a pan
  3. Cut up tofu into squares and add to pan
  4. Cut up onions
  5. Add sauce and spices to pan
  6. Mix tofu until all sides are golden brown
  7. Add onions to pan
  8. Take mixture out of pan let cool and enjoy!
