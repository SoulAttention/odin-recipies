Sourdough Toast

Sourdough is probably the most delicious thing I have ever tasted. So savory, so perfect.
It is also probiotic like yogurt so it is good for your digestive system!
Toasting sourdough is a simple step to bring it to an orgasmic level of yummy.
And once it is toasted you can put anything on top like avocado, hummus, veggies, or peanut butter.

Ingredients

  • sourdough bread
  • butter
  • oil
  • any other toppings

Directions

  1. Heat up oil in pan
  2. Place two slices of sourdough in the pan
  3. Wait till on side is crispy golden brown then flip
  4. Wait till the other side is cripspy golden brown then take out of pan
  5. Add butter and any other toppings you would like and enjoy!
